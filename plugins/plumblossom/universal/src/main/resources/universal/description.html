Plum Blossom I Ching

Universal Analysis

It fits the best for concrete questions or situations which can be expressed using a keyword. It can be used as substitute for the card method.

Usage:

  • Analysis of a momentary situation for a person
  • Background of a meeting with a person
  • Basic subject matter of a relationship, partnership, business or marriage
  • Searching for a lost item
  • Appraisal of a new job
  • Weather forecasting for a particular intention
