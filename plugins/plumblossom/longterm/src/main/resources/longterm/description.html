Plum Blossom I Ching

Longterm analysis and trends

Analysis of circumstance which is out of our limited sight. It is used for longterm predictions, for instance when we need to know future of the family, agricultural or social situtation of the globe, etc. It can also be used for prediction of tendency in marriage, business or any kind of relationship.

Using this method it is possible to predict political development or progress in an education.

Usage:

  • Simple analysis of name, place, etc.
  • Name analysis in connection to place, job, education, partner, place of living, company, etc.
  • Family constellation
